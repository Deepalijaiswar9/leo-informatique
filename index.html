About Us

Leo Infotech first began in 1992 in the form of Leo Systems – founded by Abid Ginwala and later joined by Asif Surangiwala, both of them Electronics Engineers. With an extensive background of computer architecture and industry knowledge, Leo Systems set about creating a business through which to channel their knowledge to help the ordinary person better interact with their computing investment by allowing the system to reach it’s full potential. This involved both software and hardware troubleshooting and regular maintenance

Eventually the business grew to a point where expansion was necessary and the decision was made in 1995 to transfer to a new company Leo Infotech. Leo Infotech (LI) offered Sales, Service and Repair on all makes of Desktop and Laptop Computers. By dealing honestly with customers, taking an after the sale customer service focus, and guaranteeing same day service, LI has established its reputation as one of the premier computer organizations in Mumbai specializing in repairs and building high quality custom-built new PCs.

Our team of highly qualified field engineers help us maintain our reputation in these fields. They also provide the customer with sound technical knowledge, so as to enable the customer to sort small problems by themselves.

Our office & workshop is situated at ideal location in Byculla (E), equidistant from Byculla & Mahalaxmi railway station. Also all buses to north & south of Mumbai pass through this point. Hence dispatching engineers, call-coordination to anywhere in Mumbai becomes very efficient & convenient. We have a call co-ordinator which supports & direct all field engineers.

Where we are now?

Servicing is still one of our goals, but our primary focus is now on providing the consumer with high-quality computing goods, as quickly and easily as possible. After the retirement of partners & closure of Leo Infotech, the business is now run under new name LEO INFORMATIQUE from our office at 505 Veena Killedar Industrial Estate, Byculla (W), Mumbai. Sales and maintenance is one of our highest priorities and we have a number of staff completely dedicated to this task . We have found the most effective way of providing fast service to minimize the down time.
